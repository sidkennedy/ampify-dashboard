**Effective upon acceptance.** By checking "I have read and agree" during onboarding (or signing below), the clinic identified at sign-up ("**Customer**" or "**Clinic**") agrees to this Clinic Services Agreement ("**Agreement**") with **[Ampify LLC]** ("**Ampify**," "we," "us"). The individual accepting represents that they are authorized to bind the Clinic.

## 1. The Services
Ampify provides a software platform that helps the Clinic verify patient insurance and manage the related revenue cycle, which may include: insurance **eligibility & benefits verification**, **insurance coverage discovery**, **claim status tracking**, **claims submission**, and **electronic remittance (ERA)** — delivered electronically and/or via automated telephone calls to payers (collectively, the "**Services**"). Features may be enabled or disabled per Clinic.

## 2. Authorization — Customer appoints Ampify as its agent (KEY)
The Clinic **authorizes and appoints Ampify (and its subcontractors, including its clearinghouse) to act as the Clinic's agent and authorized representative** to, on the Clinic's behalf:
- (a) **enroll the Clinic's provider identifiers** (NPI, Tax ID/EIN) with payers, clearinghouses, and transaction networks as needed to deliver the Services;
- (b) **submit and receive electronic healthcare transactions** — including eligibility (270/271), insurance discovery, claim status (276/277), claims (837), and remittance (835) — to and from payers on the Clinic's behalf; and
- (c) place automated verification telephone calls to payers identifying the Clinic as the calling provider's office.

The Clinic **represents and warrants** that: it is a validly licensed/credentialed healthcare provider; the provider information it supplies (NPI, Tax ID, addresses) is accurate and belongs to the Clinic; and it has the authority to grant the authorizations above. The Clinic will sign any payer- or government-specific enrollment forms (e.g., **Medicare/CMS EDI Enrollment Agreements**) that require the provider's own signature.

## 3. Protected Health Information (HIPAA)
In performing the Services, Ampify will create, receive, maintain, or transmit Protected Health Information ("**PHI**") on the Clinic's behalf, making Ampify a **Business Associate** of the Clinic. The parties' handling of PHI is governed by the **Business Associate Agreement (BAA)**, which is incorporated into this Agreement by reference and which the Clinic also accepts at onboarding. Ampify maintains BAAs with its own subcontractors that handle PHI.

## 4. Customer responsibilities
The Clinic is responsible for: (a) the accuracy of patient, provider, and claim data it submits; (b) maintaining its own payer credentialing and provider enrollment standing; (c) **reviewing the results** the Services return and exercising professional judgment before relying on them for clinical, billing, or financial decisions; and (d) compliance with all laws applicable to its practice, including proper coding and billing.

## 5. Fees
